Project

The Prison Management System(Smart Prison) is a web and mobile app that centralizes prisoner data, including profiles, parole, and visitation history. It tracks prison activities like food and vegetable sales. The system also includes a security feature with motion detection to monitor prisoners and trigger alarms during escape attempts, enhancing efficiency and security. The system streamlines management processes, improving efficiency and security within the prison.
